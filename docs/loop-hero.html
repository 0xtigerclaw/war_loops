Anyone can loop.
A loop is only as good as its measure.

Every pass, a loop asks one thing: better, or worse? Give it a blunt answer and it spins in place, busy and blind. Give it a sharp one and it climbs. That sharp answer is the Benchmark loop: the core driver of the whole stack, and the only thing that turns motion into progress.

measure better→experiment more→research itself

The sharper the measure, the more War Loops can safely try. And trying, against a real measure, is how a system begins to research itself. The question was never whether to loop. It was always this: what counts as "well"?

Loops inside loops, all the way down. War Loops turns any live page into an editable design, autonomously.
